THANK YOU for considering to contribute to our project and to OUR planet. This fundraising-idea was initiated as part of our start-up challenge at Chalmers School of Entrepreneurship. Our mission is to raise as much money as possible during this coming week. In order to actually generate value for more people than just ourselves, we decided to combine this with the very real crisis in part of the Amazon-forest. We need your help to win the start-up challenge but your main reason to help us should be to also help the planet. All collected funds will go to the organization Rädda Regnskog that operates to protect our rainforests through various projects. For more information about the organization and their projects visit their website here: https://raddaregnskog.se We will continuously update our webpage to show how much money we actually raise. As a token of our gratitude we will together with https://raddaregnskog.se post a certificate on our combined social channels in order to show our appreciation. This will be posted on thursday afternoon around 3 PM, so be sure to make your contribution in time! :D
